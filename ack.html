Turing AI Computing Cloud / TRS Project
Notes for Acknowledgement

Turing AI Computing Cloud (TACC) is a cloud platform for research and education in machine learning with open access to the research community. All uses of TACC should comply with rules set forth in the Term of Service.

Acknowledgement

If your work is supported by the TRS project:

"This paper is supported in part by Hong Kong RGC TRS T41-603/20-R. We would like to thank the Turing AI Computing Cloud (TACC) [cite TACC whitepaper here] and HKUST iSING Lab for providing us computation resources on their platform."

If your work is supported by the TRS project and you are a member of HKUST iSING Lab:

"This paper is supported in part by Hong Kong RGC TRS T41-603/20-R, and the Turing AI Computing Cloud (TACC) [cite TACC whitepaper here]."

If your work is not supported by the TRS project but benifits from TACC:

"We would like to thank the Turing AI Computing Cloud (TACC) [cite TACC whitepaper here] and HKUST iSING Lab for providing us computation resources on their platform."

Kindly notify us through email (tacc-contact@lists.ust.hk) when you acknowledge us in your published paper, so we can make a record of it.
