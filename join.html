TACC is a cloud platform for research and education in machine learning with open access to the HKUST research community.

Sponsored by RGC Theme-based project, TACC aims to support research work on a wide range of AI applications with high-performance and scalable infrastructure on both software- and hardware-level.

TACC equips with 128x Nvidia RTX 3090 GPUs across 16 servers, interconnected by high-performance RDMA network.

You are invited to our workshop on how to run your machine learning tasks on TACC with step-by-step tutorials and accelerate your experiments with best practices.

Dive deeper into these topics:
  • Running large-scale data-parallel tasks on TACC
  • Uploading your own dataset and retrieving results
  • Managing third-party code dependencies
  • Troubleshooting your ML task
