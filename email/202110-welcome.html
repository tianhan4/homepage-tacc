TACC Resource Specs and Limitations
 
TACC employs the following hardware:
  • GPU: 16 GPU servers, each with 8 RTX3090 GPUs, 256GB DDR4 memory, 40 cores Intel Xeon Gold series CPU, 1TB SSD, and 2 Mellanox Connect X5 100Gbps dual port.
  • Storage: 5 storage nodes, each with 128GB DDR4 memory, 40 cores Intel Xeon Gold series CPU, 32TB SSD, and 1 Mellanox Connect X5 100Gbps dual port.
  • CPU: 5 CPU nodes, each with 32GB DDR4 memory, 40 cores Intel Xeon Gold series CPU, 1TB SSD, and 1 Mellanox Connect X5 100Gbps dual port.
  • Network: 4 Mellanox SN2700 switches.
 
Usage Limits
Please comply with the current resource usage limitations:
  1. One account is allowed to submit up to two jobs at a time.
  2. A maximum of 2 computing nodes may be occupied by each job at the same time.
  3. Each occupied computing node must not remain idle for more than 8 hours.
  4. Do not fill up all the memory on a computing node or run a large number of processes that occupy the CPUs.
  5. Each account's home directory has a maximum quota of 100GB. If additional storage space is required, please contact the administrator (WeChat ID: mini-Coke).
