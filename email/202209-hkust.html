Introducing TACC for AI Computing
 
Dear Faculties and Students,

I am glad to introduce the Turing AI Computing Cloud (TACC) to you. TACC is a cloud platform for research and education in machine learning with open access to the HKUST research community.

Sponsored by the Theme-based project, TACC aims to support research work on a wide range of machine learning applications with high-performance and scalable infrastructure on both software- and hardware-level.

Since TACC first launched in May 2021 within the CSE department, over 170 CSE faculties/students have used the platform to run their ML experiments: in total, there were 9,177 jobs submitted to TACC, and they have run 89,000+ GPU hours. Right now, TACC has 128x Nvidia RTX 3090 GPUs across 16 servers interconnected by high-performance RDMA networking.



If you haven't tried TACC, you are welcome to read more about TACC's features as well as applying for an account on tacc.ust.hk. We sincerely hope TACC can accelerate your ML and AI tasks.

Best Regards,
Kai Chen
